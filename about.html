Who I Am

About Me

I'm Connor — a finance and crypto professional based between Toronto and Parry Sound, Ontario. I've spent the last several years working in Canada's digital asset industry, helping high-net-worth individuals and institutions navigate crypto markets.

Currently serving as Director of Sales & Trading at Bitbuy, one of Canada's leading crypto exchanges, I bring a wealth management mindset to the world of digital assets.

Outside of work, my life revolves around golf, fitness, and an obsession with longevity and human performance. I'm particularly passionate about peptides and their role in optimizing health, recovery, and performance — a topic I love to research and share.

I'm also teaching myself to code and build websites — this site is part of that journey.
